Frequently
Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about
our services.

How does Uber Ride works in Keny?
One orders a ride at a tap of a button and get picked up by a
top-rated driver in more than 600 cities in the country.
How do i request a ride?
Visit the Uber Ride site, enter your destination and
you will be matched with a local driver.
How long will it take to get a ride?
Once you have requested for a ride, car usually arrives in a minute, but waiting
time may vary depending on the distance.
Is there an extra fee from Uber Ride?
No, you can see the final price in the site before requesting a ride.
How do i contact Uber Ride in Kenya?
You can reach out to their support team through site or via email.
What are benefits for joining Uber Ride?
One has the opportunity to order ride which are fast, safe and secure.
